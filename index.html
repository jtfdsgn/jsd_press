ABOUT

Hello, I'm Jeremy Stock, owner and operator of JSD Pressworks.

I have worked as a Graphic Designer in the Tampa Bay area for over 15 years now, working under the label JStock Designs. I began working by commission only, but overtime evolved JStock Designs into an apparel brand which can be found in many local tackle shops and boating stores. As I invested more time into the apparel brand, I focused further on production, printing all my products to ensure the best quality and control. This marked the beginning of JSD Pressworks.

Having developed the best practices while printing my own merchandise, JSD Pressworks offers clients a high-quality, yet cost-effective product. Nothing can be more off-putting than a company with shoddy merchandise, which is way we hold a particularly high standard for quality control.

Whether you need shirts, microfibers, hats, stickers, or any other merchandise printed, silkscreened, embroidered, or sublimated, we've got you covered. We print for tackle shops, apparel companies, boating stores, little league teams, charter captains, gyms, festivals, coffee shops, city municipalities, and boat manufacturers, to name a few.

No job is too small, if you have a need, we have a solution. Whether you have print-ready artwork, or a sketch on a napkin, our in-house designer can get your artwork ready and we'll get it printed in no time!

SERVICES

DIRECT-TO-GARMENT

A process of printing an image directly onto fabric. This process is designed for use with cotton shirts, and ideal for small runs. There are no limits regarding the number of colors used.

SUBLIMATION

This process is ideal for applying full color designs to performance materials, such as microfiber and polyester shirts. It uses a heat press to turn the design into a gas, which is absorbed by the shirt. Creating a personalized product with soft hand feel that will never fade or crack.

EMBROIDERY

We offer a variety of embroidery products, the most common being designs embroidered onto hats, patches, and polos. Once the patches are completely, they can be applied to any number of items.

DESIGN

JSD Pressworks also has a full service design department. Not only do we make sure your artwork prints correctly across various methods, we also offer design services to create your future designs.

VINYL DECALS

We offer both single-color diecut decals and full color printed sticker services, and will help you decide which style is best for each instance.

SCREENPRINTING

Screenprinting offers an economical option for large-quantity orders, and produces a great product. This process is ideal for large orders of either simple one-color designs, or four-color artwork.
