SERVICES

DIRECT-TO-GARMENT

A process of printing an image directly onto fabric. This process is ideal for small runs of cotton shirts, and poses no limits to the number of colors used.

SUBLIMATION

This process is ideal for applying full color designs to performance materials, such as microfiber and polyester shirts. It uses a heat press to turn the design into a gas, which is absorbed by the shirt. Creating a personalized product with no trace of the printing while wearing the shirt.

EMBROIDERY

We offer a variety of embroidery products, the most common being designs embroidered onto hats and patches, the latter of which can be applied to a variety of articles.

DESIGN

JSD Pressworks also has a full service design department. Not only do we make sure your artwork prints correctly across various methods, we also offer design services to create your future designs.

VINYL DECALS

We offer both single-color diecut decals and full color printed sticker services, and will help you decide which style is best for each instance.
